About Me

Hello! Read more about me as a blossoming, junior developer.

I was raised in Cincinnati, OH and went to Ball State University to study music in Muncie, IN. After living in Indianapolis, I moved to Washington, DC with my current partner. After 3 years we decided to moved to our current home in Chicago, IL where I currently work at Northwestern University as a Talent Acquisition Partner.

When I'm not working as a Human Resources professional, I enjoy spending time with my husband of 2 years and our cat and golden retriever. I love playing video games, finding ways to stay fit, cooking (and subsequently eating) delicious food, and playing intermural sports around the Chicago area.

I find myself attracted to Information Technology because, for lack of a better term, it's the future! I've always been a creative person and attracted to different methods of self-expression. With IT, I can allow myself to be creative and still stay on top of growing trends in the development field.
